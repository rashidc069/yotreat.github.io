About Us

Yotreat, as the name suggests, is a fresh and young thought on how the traditional snacks should be prepared and presented. We started off with a mission to produce ready-to-eat traditional snacks and savours with highest standard and serve them in exotic packages. At Yotreat, we take the utmost care to employ fresh and quality ingredients to prepare a variety of our crispy and tasty snacks. We don't compromise at all on the hygienic factors while producing or packaging our products.
